CamelCase: Words are concatenated, with each word after the first beginning with a capital letter.

snake_case: Words are separated by underscores, all lowercase.

kebab-case: Words are separated by hyphens, all lowercase.

PascalCase: Like CamelCase, but with the first letter of each word capitalized.

UPPERCASE: All letters capitalized, words may be separated by underscore
